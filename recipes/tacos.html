Tacos

Since tacos are one of the most versatile and tasty meals, today, we are going to learn the basics of making tacos.

So get ready your comal and let's start!

    Ingredients
  • Tortillas
  • Beans
  • Cheese
  • Valentina sauce

Now, let's start with the most basic of all, bean tacos.

    Steps
  1. Put the beans and some cheese inside the tortilla and fold it.
  2. Put the tortilla on a preheated comal and wait until the beans are hot.
  3. Take the taco and open it. Add a bit of Valentina sauce and enjoy!
